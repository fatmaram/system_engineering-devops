In this project, I explored one of the most fundamental aspects of the Linux shell: input/output redirections and text-processing filters. My goal was to understand how data flows in and out of commands, how to control where output goes, and how to combine simple tools to perform powerful operations.

I worked with commands like cat, head, tail, sort, and uniq, and practiced redirecting both standard output and error streams. I also experimented with pipes to chain commands together, creating efficient workflows for handling text data.
